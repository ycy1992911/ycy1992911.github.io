ARTS Punch In Schedule

Apr 22, 2019

What is ARTS?

It stands for Algorithm, Review, Tips and Share.

It is a weekly schedule to improve and share.

Algorithm:

Get a problem done on leetcode.com, with Test Driven Development.

Review:

Review a technical article on the big tech companies’ tech blogs, or some other sites like Medium.com.

Tip:

Summarise the key points in the work life. They can be problems solved during work, the difficulties I have been through or the new knowledge I’ve learned.

Share:

To speak out my own voice and opinions, and share an article with unique perspectives and insightful thinking.
